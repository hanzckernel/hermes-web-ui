# History and Search
![History Sessions](assets/screenshots/history-sessions.png)

History and Search allows you to retrieve past conversations, resume previous work, and find specific information across all your recorded sessions.

## What you can do here
* Browse recent sessions and open a specific conversation.
* Search by title, content, or relevant terms.
* Use deep links to return to a session view.
* Distinguish saved history from the currently open chat session.

## Typical workflow
When you need to resume a previous task, open the history view to browse recent sessions. If the session isn't immediately visible, use the global search modal to find it by keyword or title. Once found, click the session to open it and review the past context before continuing your work. 

## Key controls
* **Session List:** Scroll through chronologically ordered past conversations.
* **Global Search:** Enter keywords to find specific messages or sessions.
* **Deep Links:** Share or bookmark specific session states.

## Current history behavior

History navigation has been tuned for longer conversations. Use the sidebar and loading controls to move through older sessions deliberately instead of assuming the full history list is loaded at once.

## Notes and limits
* Search is useful for recall, but stale sessions may not reflect current files or settings.
* Use the session content as context; verify current project state before acting on old instructions.
